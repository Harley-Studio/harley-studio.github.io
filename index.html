About Harley Studio

Founded in 2025, we're passionate about developing technology that makes life more engaging and connected

Building digital solutions that enhance everyday life and bring people together.

Harley Studio is an innovative Australian startup creating digital solutions that enhance everyday life and bring people together. We believe in the power of thoughtfully designed technology to improve lives, build connections, and foster meaningful experiences.

  • Community-focused applications that bring people together
  • Thoughtfully designed user experiences that enhance daily life
  • Starting with sports and expanding to solve problems across different areas of interest

We're passionate about developing apps that make life more engaging and connected. Starting with community sports through our flagship app Harley Meets, we have exciting plans for additional applications that will help people in new and meaningful ways.

Our Flagship App

Harley Meets

A sports event management platform that makes it easier for people to create, discover, and join local pickup sports events

Create Events

Easily organize pickup sports events in your community. Set the time, location, and sport - let others know you're ready to play.

Discover Local Games

Find pickup sports events happening near you. Whether you're into badminton, pickleball, yoga, or gymnastics - there's something for everyone.

Connect & Play

Join events with like-minded people in your community. Build connections through sports and stay active together.
